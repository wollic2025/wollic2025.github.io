Porto is a fascinating and vibrant city that has so much to offer as a holiday or city break destination. The city boasts an extensive history, unique tourist attractions, delicious cuisines and buzzing nightlife. Porto has a delightful historic center and is famed for its Port wine, but it is also a young and energetic city, with social nightlife, liberal attitudes and a flourishing artisan scene. Take a look at [this page](https://porto-north-portugal.com) for a quick guide of the city. The conference venue is the **Department of Computer Science of the University of Porto** and lunches are served at the **Círculo Universitário do Porto**.

### Getting There...

The nearest **airport** to the city of Porto is the [**Sá Carneiro Airtport**](https://www.portoairport.pt/en/opo/home). From there, you can pick one of many efficient public transportation networks at your service on [this page](https://www.portoairport.pt/en/opo/access-parking/getting-to-and-from-the-airport/public-transportation) to get to the venue.

We **recommend** taking [**metro line E**](https://www.metrodoporto.pt/metrodoporto/uploads/document/file/471/mapa_de_rede.pdf) at the [**Botica**](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Ptzt7FBzgu1UHkZj6) metro station (located near the airport), and exiting at the [**Casa da Música**](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Gm31VVZcCVe35fup9) metro station. From there, you can either: **walk** to the [**venue**](https://maps.app.goo.gl/9x8aPVrpP67SxG9Q7) (aprox. 21 min); or **take** the **204** or **209** **bus line** available at the [**Casa da Música (Metro)**](https://maps.app.goo.gl/zAzWsKbPgXgX8XSu9) bus station and exiting at the [**Faculdade de Ciências**](https://maps.app.goo.gl/w5akohJYTGuZQXxv5) bus station. From there, you will find clear instruction guiding you to the [**venue**](https://maps.app.goo.gl/NGf2FNJLYzSWCK7c7). **Lunches** will be served at the [**Círculo Universitário do Porto**](https://maps.app.goo.gl/rMyojFJLTR7qU5gbA).

You can find the location of the airport, all relevant metro and bus stations, and the venue on the map below. In order to navigate around the city center, we recommend using your favorite GPS navigation app.

For further information, please contact one of the local organizers (after the map).
